UI and UX are two critical components of a digital product. This workshop will cover the basics of UI and UX design. We will discuss how to develop a strategy for creating a good user interface and making sure that your users’ needs are satisfied. We’ll also discuss common pitfalls in UI/UX design and how to avoid them. This will cover various topics including the importance of user research, wire framing, prototyping and testing your designs with users. At the end of the workshop, participants will have a firm understanding of the key concepts of UI and UX design as well as a general sense of how these concepts can be applied in their own work.
