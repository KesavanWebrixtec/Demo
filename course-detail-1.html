About Course

Designing an effective user experience starts with understanding user needs. With Figma, Adobe XD. learn how designers can quickly create prototypes and wireframes that provide insight into how people interact with an application. You’ll explore best practices for improving usability and accessibility through interaction design and discover how to incorporate data analytics into your workflow.
